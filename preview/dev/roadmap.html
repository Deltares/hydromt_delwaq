Roadmap#

The HydroMT-delwaq package currently supports the D-Emissions / D-Water Quality version coupled to the 2D hydrological model Wflow. Our ambition is to further extend the HydroMT-delwaq to support more software packages that makes use of the DELWAQ Process Library, such as the Delft3D Suite, SOBEK Suite and D-HYDRO Suite 1D2D.
